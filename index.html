About IDR

Where Academic
Insight Meets
Industry Practice


The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute built for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments. We exist at the intersection of rigorous academic research and the operational demands of sectors where risk has real consequences.

Through our UK university partnership, IDR students and professionals gain access to cutting-edge research, accredited curricula and live industry projects — ensuring every graduate is not just trained, but ready to deploy on day one.

What We Do

Three Pillars.
One Mission.

IDR operates across training, innovation and advisory — a fully integrated model that takes practitioners from qualification to real-world deployment.

01 / Academy

IDR Academy

Structured bootcamps, professional development tracks and accredited programmes that produce job-ready digital and cyber risk practitioners. Delivered in partnership with leading UK universities, our curriculum blends theory, live simulation and real industry case studies.

BootcampsDegree PathwaysCPDCertification
02 / Innovation

Innovation & Incubation

IDR develops proprietary IP, future-forward risk models and AI governance frameworks with industry partners. Our incubation pipeline turns research into deployable tools — from AI risk scoring to regulatory automation and digital-twin threat modelling.

AI GovernanceIP DevelopmentRisk ModelsR&D
03 / Advisory

Advisory Services

IDR embeds deployment-ready practitioners inside client organisations to implement and operationalise industry-standard frameworks. We support NIST CSF, ISO 27001, NIS2 and emerging AI regulation — built around real operational context, not just documentation.
